Welcome to L. N. Maity International Ayurvedic Medical Research Foundation

Welcome to L. N. Maity International Ayurvedic Medical Research Foundation, a pioneer in the field of Ayurvedic medicine and herbal products. Established in 2013 in the heart of West Bengal, India, our foundation is driven by a passion to deliver high-quality, chemical-free Ayurvedic solutions for better health and well-being.

Founded and led by Dr. Lakshmi Narayan Maity, a highly experienced Ayurvedic practitioner with over 50 years of experience in Ayurvedic medicine preparation, our mission is to provide the most authentic, pure, and effective herbal products globally. Dr. Maity holds an MD in Ayurvedic Medicine (MD(Ay)), and his vast expertise is reflected in every product we create.
